Mission Statement

At Gru, we believe that eating well shouldn't mean sacrificing flavor or enjoyment. Our mission is simple: to empower you to make informed choices about your nutrition while indulging in delicious meals that nourish both body and soul. Whether you're a seasoned chef or just starting your culinary journey, our innovative Nutrition Calculator is here to guide you. Simply enter your ingredients, and watch as a world of wholesome recipes unfolds before your eyes. Access your recipe book at the click of a button and easily view nutrition information and recipe book statistics. Join us on this journey towards a healthier, happier you. Let's cook up a storm of good health together!


Nutritional Analysis

Our app uses nutrition analysis technology to quickly analyze any recipe that you input, giving you all the neccssary nutritional facts within seconds.

Recipe Box

Keep all of your recipes in one spot at Gru! Every recipe that you input gets stored in your own personalized recipe box that can be accessed at any time.

Recipe Statistics

Every recipe analysis generates recipe tags that can make organizing your recipes super simple! We have implemented a recipe box analysis feature which allows you to get a glimpse on the types of recipes you have collected so far!
